# Product-Management-Platforms-Portfolio

## Overview
This repository is a curated **Product Management portfolio** showcasing 0→1 product strategy, platform design, analytics-led decisioning, and lifecycle-driven growth systems across **creator commerce, affiliate platforms, payments, onboarding, and dashboards**.

The work here reflects ownership across the **entire product lifecycle** — from discovery and market research to PRDs, UX flows, experimentation, analytics, and scalable execution — with a strong focus on **B2B2C platforms, monetisation, retention loops, and compliance-safe financial flows**.

### Project 01 — XLEY Affiliate Platform (0→1 White-Label Launch)
**Files**
- `XLEY_FINAL_FLOW.pdf`
- `XLEY_Profit_Share.pdf`

**Problem**
Creators lacked scalable tools to monetise content beyond manual links, while brands needed measurable creator-led performance.

**Solution**
Designed a full-stack creator affiliate platform with:
- AutoDM-based distribution
- Profit-sharing & boost mechanics
- Campaign & earnings dashboards
- Scalable onboarding & payouts

**Key Deliverables**
- PRDs & MVP scope definition
- AutoDM trigger logic & keyword flows
- Profit-share toggles & incentive UX
- Performance analytics & withdrawal flows

**Outcome**
- Clear monetisation roadmap
- Retention-first creator journeys
- Enterprise-ready white-label platform design

### Project 02 — Payments & Withdrawal Experience (Compliance-Safe)
**File**
- `EK Payments Withdrawal.pdf`

**Focus**
Designed a **secure, compliant payout experience** for creators and partners.

**Key Features**
- KYC-driven document collection (PAN, Aadhaar, GST)
- Status-based progression & retry handling
- Payment method selection (UPI / Bank Transfer)
- Clear failure states & resolution messaging

**Impact**
- Reduced payout friction
- Improved trust & transparency
- Scalable reconciliation-ready design
